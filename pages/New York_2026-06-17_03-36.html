New York

Statue of Liberty

Take a ferry to Liberty Island to visit the iconic Statue of Liberty, a symbol of freedom and democracy.

Central Park

Escape the hustle and bustle of Manhattan in this 843-acre green oasis, offering walking paths, lakes, and plenty of people-watching opportunities.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

One of the world's largest and most renowned museums, featuring a vast collection of art and artifacts from around the globe.
