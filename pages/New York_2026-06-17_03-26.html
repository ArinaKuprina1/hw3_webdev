Top Attractions

Statue of Liberty

A iconic symbol of freedom and democracy, the Statue of Liberty is a must-visit attraction in New York City.

Central Park

A peaceful oasis in the middle of Manhattan, Central Park offers a variety of activities, including walking, picnicking, and boating.

Empire State Building

An iconic skyscraper and one of the tallest buildings in the world, the Empire State Building offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks.
