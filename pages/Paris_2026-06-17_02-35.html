Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower

One of the most iconic landmarks in the world, the Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris. You can take the stairs or elevator to the top for breathtaking views of the city.

Notre Dame Cathedral

This beautiful Gothic cathedral has been a symbol of Paris for over 800 years. You can take a guided tour or attend a mass to experience its beauty and history.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa, the Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums. You can spend hours exploring its vast collection of art and artifacts.
