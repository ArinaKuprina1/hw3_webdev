Top Attractions

  • Shibuya Crossing

    One of the busiest intersections in the world, known for its neon lights and giant video screens.

  • Meiji Shrine

    A serene Shinto shrine located in a tranquil forested area, dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken.

  • Imperial Palace

    The main residence of the Emperor of Japan, a large park-like area with beautiful gardens and architecture.
