Top Attractions

The Eiffel Tower

One of the most iconic landmarks in the world, the Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris. Take the elevator to the top for breathtaking views of the city.

Musée d'Orsay

Home to an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art, the Musée d'Orsay is one of the world's greatest museums. Don't miss the iconic Monet's Water Lilies.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic cathedral that dates back to the 12th century, Notre Dame is a must-visit for anyone interested in history, architecture, or faith.
