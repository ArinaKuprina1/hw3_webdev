Top Attractions

Here are the top attractions in Amsterdam:

  • Rijksmuseum

    The Rijksmuseum is one of the most famous museums in the world, housing an impressive collection of Dutch art and history.

  • Van Gogh Museum

    The Van Gogh Museum is dedicated to the life and works of Vincent Van Gogh, featuring the largest collection of his paintings and letters.

  • Ann Frank House

    The Anne Frank House is a museum dedicated to the Jewish girl who wrote a diary during WWII, hiding from the Nazis in Amsterdam.
