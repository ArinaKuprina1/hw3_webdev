Paris Travel Guide

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic iron lattice tower located in the heart of Paris, France. Built for the 1889 World's Fair, it stands at an impressive 324 meters tall and offers stunning views of the city.

Notre Dame Cathedral

Notre Dame Cathedral is a beautiful Gothic church located on the Île de la Cité in the heart of Paris. It was built in the 12th century and features stunning stained glass windows and a magnificent rose window.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, located in the heart of Paris. It features an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Mona Lisa.
