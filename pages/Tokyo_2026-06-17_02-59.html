Top Attractions

  • Shibuya Crossing

    One of the busiest intersection in the world, famous for its scramble crossing and neon lights.

  • Meiji Shrine

    A serene Shinto shrine located in a peaceful forested area, dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken.

  • Tokyo Skytree

    The tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.
