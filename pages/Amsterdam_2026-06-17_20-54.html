Amsterdam

Discover the charming city of Amsterdam, known for its beautiful canals, rich history, and vibrant culture.

Rijksmuseum

The Rijksmuseum is a must-visit attraction in Amsterdam, featuring an impressive collection of Dutch art and history, including works by Rembrandt and Vermeer.

Anne Frank House

The Anne Frank House is a museum dedicated to the Jewish girl who wrote a diary during WWII, hiding from the Nazis in Amsterdam. The house has been converted into a museum, showcasing the Secret Annex where Anne Frank lived.

Van Gogh Museum

The Van Gogh Museum is home to the largest collection of Vincent van Gogh's paintings and letters, offering a unique insight into the life and work of the famous Dutch artist.
