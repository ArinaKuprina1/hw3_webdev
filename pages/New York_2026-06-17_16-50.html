New York

Statue of Liberty

Located on Liberty Island, the Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy, and one of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

Central Park

Central Park is an 843-acre green oasis in the middle of Manhattan, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city.

9/11 Memorial & Museum

The 9/11 Memorial & Museum is a poignant tribute to the victims of the 9/11 terrorist attacks, featuring two massive reflecting pools surrounded by the names of those who were killed.
